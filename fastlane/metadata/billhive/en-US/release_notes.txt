What's new in 1.9.1:

• Reliability — edits are now safely persisted even when the app is backgrounded mid-edit. If your save file is corrupt, the new edit is parked in a recovery file instead of overwriting month history
• Trends accuracy — historical totals stay correct after you edit bills or remove a person, and remainder lines display correctly in the per-month breakdown
• Notification fixes — same-day reminders no longer get silently dropped after 9 AM, and triggers handle DST and timezone changes correctly
• Performance — Summary and Trends views are noticeably faster on larger households
