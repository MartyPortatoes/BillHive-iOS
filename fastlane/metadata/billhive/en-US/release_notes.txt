What's new in 2.0.1:

Bug fixes & polish:
• Fixed a crash when removing a bill from the editor
• Fixed date picker text wrapping on smaller screens (iPhone 15 Pro and similar)
• Fixed "Awaiting" and "Paid" status badges getting cut off on smaller screens
• Confirmation popups (import backup, remove bill) now use a consistent centered alert style

UI improvements:
• Trends donut chart now shows a per-person breakdown — tap a segment to see their name, percentage, and amount
• Added descriptive subtitles to the Trends and Settings tabs
• Consistent title positioning and spacing across all tabs
• More compact date picker with reduced padding

Performance:
• Faster iCloud sync with off-main-thread writes and debounced scheduling
• Batched per-month writes to reduce disk I/O in local mode
