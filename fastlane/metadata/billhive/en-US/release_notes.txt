What's new in 2.0.0:

• Paid By — bills can now be assigned to any household member, not just you. Net balances compute automatically so you can see who owes whom
• You Owe — new summary section shows what you owe others, with per-bill breakdowns and paid/unpaid status
• Between Others — view third-party settlements between other household members
• Import from Contacts — add people directly from your address book with name, email, and phone pre-filled
• Rounding fix — percentage splits now distribute cents correctly (no more $50.01 on a 3-way $150 split)
• Checklist improvements — "Pay" items turn green with strikethrough when marked done
• Refreshed app icons
